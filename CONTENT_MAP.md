# Content map for the final public site

## Keep

1. Exact paper title and public author list.
2. One-sentence summary.
3. Four resource buttons: paper, supplement, arXiv, code.
4. Three short overview cards.
5. One method schematic or the high-resolution main framework figure.
6. Three headline metrics.
7. One compact generator table and one compact policy table.
8. Two paired qualitative demos.
9. BibTeX and corresponding-author contact.

## Remove from the current page

- Separate long motivation section.
- Full abstract duplicated from the paper.
- Architecture map plus multiple architecture figures.
- Full-width tables with every metric.
- Training curves.
- Component heatmap.
- Eight “key number” cards that repeat the tables.
- The 16 scenario GIFs and 12 policy GIFs.
- Anonymous citation placeholder.
- “Anonymous Project Preview” and “Under Review” wording.

## Recommended assets

- Method: one clean main framework figure.
- Scenario demo: one representative logged/generated pair.
- Policy demo: one representative baseline/risk-gated pair.
- Optional: one scorer calibration plot only if it remains readable on mobile.
